# **\[v1.6\] Empires Mode (Coming Soon)**

Each player is enable to choose their empire, which giving buff to 1 specific Dice type unit

- Dice 1: Greek Sparta  
  - Armor \+1  
  - Reduce that enemy's Armor by 1 **before** combat, adjacent enemy  
- Dice 2: Indian Apache  
  - Attack \+1  
  - Ignore terrain movement costs, ignore enemy obstacle on movement  
- Dice 3: Mongolian Cavalry  
  - After a Dice 3 unit successfully defeats an enemy unit in combat, it may immediately move up to 1 hex to an empty adjacent hex  
  - Can jump over **enemy** (normally only friendly) units during their movement  
- Dice 4: Soviet Red Tank  
  - Armor \+1  
  - When your Dice 4 units attack and fail, they apply 2 Armor Reduction tokens to the defender instead of 1\.  
- Dice 5: British LongBow  
  - When in Guard Mode, Range \+1, remove Guard mode after an attack  
- Dice 6: Roman Legionnaire  
  - Adjacent friendly units (including the Dice 6 itself) gain **\+1 Armor** and **recover 1 Armor Reduction token** at the start of the Dice 6 owner's turn  
  - When your Dice 6 unit performs Terraform, the terrain change is immediate
