  The 3-Spell Kit Analysis:
   1. Shield (Add Guard): Increases the "Effective Armor" of a frontline unit without
      them having to waste their own turn Guarding.
   2. Swap (Positional): The ultimate survival and mobility tool. Can swap with any
      friendly unit within 2 hexes.
   3. Mending (Heal): Clears "Armor Reduction." This makes the Enchanter the natural
      partner for the Tanker (5), keeping their 4 Armor pristine.

  Balance Concerns to Watch:
   * The "Double Mage" Problem: If a player rolls two 6s, can they swap two Mages? (Yes,
     but they still have 0 Armor, so it’s mostly for positioning).
   * Engaged Restriction: Should the Mage be allowed to cast while an enemy is adjacent?
       * My recommendation: Allow it. If the Mage is engaged, "Swap" is their only way to
         survive. If you prevent them from casting while engaged, they are too easy to
         kill.
   * Line of Sight: Should the Mage need to "see" the target?
       * My recommendation: Yes. Requiring Line of Sight (like the Archer) means the Mage
         can't hide behind a mountain (if you add terrain) or through a dense crowd
         without careful positioning.
